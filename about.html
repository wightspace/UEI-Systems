Industry

UEI Systems is a member company of the UEI Group. UEI Systems was established in 1992 as a solution for the engraving industry. Prior to UEI Systems, inconsistent metal, erratic chemistry and additives, outdated and inefficient machinery plagued the engraving industry. UEI Group knew there had to be a better way- so they started UEI Systems with a commitment to improve the processes, supplies and raw materials for engraving.

Innovation

Servicing many industries with our photoengraving, hand engraving and machine engraving technology, UEI Systems has been able to conquer many of the issues of the past and forge new innovative, timesaving and quality-driven processes, many of which are considered proprietary or have been patented. These advancements have not only revolutionized the engraving industry but also provided our clients with unprecedented precision and efficiency. Our patented processes are a testament to our commitment to continuous improvement and delivering the highest quality products to our customers.

Technology

Our products were developed for engravers by engravers. So we know they work and work well. We have combined a state-of-the-art team of engineers, chemists and most importantly engravers to develop our UEI Systems product line. Our team has employed proven practices in combination with new innovative, sophisticated technology to produce a product line that engravers can count on. We wanted our products to be reliable, environmentally sound, quality driven, timesaving, and useful.
